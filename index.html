About Appolo Scales

Mathew Joseph, a visionary in precision instrumentation, founded Appolo Scales in 1987 with a simple mission: to deliver reliable, accurate weighing solutions for industries, traders, and institutions across Kerala.

From our workshop in Pattithanam, Kottayam, we have grown into a trusted name, known for robust construction, digital innovation, and after‑sales commitment. Every scale we manufacture reflects our core values – honesty, precision, and Indian craftsmanship.
